Image View controls

1) zooming is done by scrolling the middle mouse button or right mouse
button click and drag up or down
2) panning is done by clicking and dragging with middle mouse button
3)  contrast is controlled by left mouse button click and drag left or right
4)  brightness is controlled by left mouse button click and drag up or down
5) pressing 'r' key resets the brightness/contrast to default
6) press Shift+r will reset the view of the image (zoom and pan) to fit in the window
